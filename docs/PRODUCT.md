# AblePath Product Baseline

**Name:** AblePath

**Chinese subtitle:** 帮助行动受限者自主使用电脑和 AI

AblePath is not framed as “giving users hands” or “fixing” a limitation. The product language should emphasize autonomy, choice, calm control, and family reassurance.

## Product Promise

- Help users control a computer through voice, gaze, switches, screen understanding, and AI assistance.
- Let users choose and use any AI product rather than being locked into one Agent SDK.
- Give caregivers status visibility and emergency notification with privacy-first defaults.

## Naming Notes

Use “AblePath” as the project name in code, docs, and UI. Avoid deficit-oriented copy such as “replace hands”, “disabled brain”, or “rescue helpless users”.
